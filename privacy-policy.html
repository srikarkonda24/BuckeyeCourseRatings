Privacy Policy

BuckeyeCourseRatings Chrome Extension · Last updated June 4, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the BuckeyeCourseRatings Chrome extension (“the Extension”) handles information when you use it. We are committed to keeping your experience simple and private.

What the Extension Does

BuckeyeCourseRatings enhances the Ohio State University class search page at classes.osu.edu by displaying RateMyProfessors ratings and reviews next to instructor names. When you hover over a rating badge, the Extension shows additional details including recent public reviews. You no longer need to open a separate tab to look up professor ratings.

Data Collection

We collect no personal data whatsoever. The Extension does not gather, store, or transmit your name, email, browsing history, course selections, search queries, or any other personally identifiable information.

Local Storage

The only data stored by the Extension is your on/off preference for showing ratings. This is saved locally on your device using chrome.storage.local and is never sent to us or any third party.

  • What is stored: whether ratings display is enabled (true/false)
  • Where it is stored: your browser only, via Chrome’s extension storage API
  • Who can access it: only the Extension on your device

Third-Party Communication

To show professor ratings, the Extension sends instructor names from the OSU class search page to ratemyprofessors.com to retrieve publicly available rating data. This communication is limited to what is necessary to display ratings and reviews. We do not add tracking identifiers or user profiles to these requests.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not track your activity across websites
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any user data
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or fingerprinting services
  • We do not require an account or login to use the Extension

Children’s Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
